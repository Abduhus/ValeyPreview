### Fragrance Care Tips
```
🌸 *Fragrance Care Tips*

Make your luxury fragrance last longer:

🏠 *Storage*
• Keep away from direct sunlight
• Store in cool, dry place
• Original box is best protection
• Avoid bathroom storage

💨 *Application*
• Apply to pulse points
• Don't rub - let it absorb
• Best on clean, moisturized skin
• 2-3 sprays are usually enough

⏰ *Best Times to Apply*
• Right after shower
• Before getting dressed
• On bare skin, not clothes
• Reapply after 6-8 hours if needed

Need specific advice for your fragrance? Send us the name and we'll share personalized tips! 💫
```
